06 — Usage Rules
Do & Don't
✓ Do
Use the dark version on white/light backgrounds
Use the white version on teal or photo backgrounds
Keep logo mark clear space = 1× icon width on all sides
Use Tactical Teal (#12CFA0) as the sole accent color
Use Inter for all text across all channels
Letter-spacing 8–14px for ALL-CAPS labels
Maintain minimum logo size: 32px wide for mark only
✗ Don't
Don't stretch or distort the logo
Don't place the dark logo on dark backgrounds
Don't recolor the logo in unauthorized colors
Don't add drop shadows or effects to the logo
Don't use the logo below 32px width
Don't mix Inter with decorative or serif fonts
Don't use low-contrast text (min 4.5:1 contrast ratio)
